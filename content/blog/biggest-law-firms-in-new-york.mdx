## What Are The 10 Biggest Law Firms In New York?

Let us break down the heavyweights. Instead of boring corporate stats, I want to give you the real inside scoop on what makes each of these places unique.

### 1\. Kirkland & Ellis

Kirkland is an absolute powerhouse. When you look at their revenue, they are practically in a league of their own. They represent the largest private equity funds in the world. If two massive global companies are joining forces, Kirkland is usually sitting at the head of the table. They employ some of the fiercest and most brilliant mergers and acquisitions lawyers nyc has to offer. The environment is highly demanding, but the financial rewards for their partners are astronomical.

### 2\. Latham & Watkins

Latham is a massive international firm with a truly global footprint. They are not just famous in Manhattan. They have offices everywhere. Working here means you get access to incredible training and endless resources. If you want to understand exactly how lawyers are paid at the absolute highest level of the industry, Latham sets a prime example. You will work incredibly long hours, but you will also work on deals that literally shape the world economy.

### 3\. Skadden, Arps, Slate, Meagher & Flom

Everyone in the industry simply calls them Skadden. They practically invented the modern corporate law firm structure as we know it today. Having the Skadden name on your resume is like having a golden ticket. It opens almost any door in the corporate world. The partner profits at top law firms like Skadden are staggering, but reaching that partnership level requires over a decade of flawless, non stop dedication.

### 4\. Paul Weiss

Paul Weiss stands out among the biggest law firms in New York for its absolute dominance in the courtroom. When major banks, huge corporations, or prominent politicians get in serious trouble, Paul Weiss is the very first number they dial. They house the most feared commercial litigation attorneys New York has ever seen. You get unparalleled courtroom experience here, but trial preparation means many sleepless nights.

![A modern boardroom inside one of the biggest law firms in new york.png](https://framerusercontent.com/images/H9uRDO5v2x7rsFfHB7BanQh3zsQ.png)

### 5\. Davis Polk & Wardwell

Davis Polk is the definition of an elite, traditional firm. They completely dominate capital markets and high level banking law. The firm offers a highly professional, pristine, and prestigious environment. It is a bit more traditional and conservative than some of the newer tech focused firms. They attract the sharpest minds in the country, boasting numerous chambers ranked lawyers nyc relies on for complex financial advice.

### 6\. Sullivan & Cromwell

This is a historic powerhouse that has been around for over a century. Sullivan represents legacy blue chip clients and monumental financial institutions that have been around for generations. You sacrifice a relaxed startup culture for immense stability and prestige. They maintain some of the most beautiful and historic Manhattan corporate law offices in the entire city.

### 7\. Simpson Thacher & Bartlett

When ranking the biggest law firms in New York, Simpson Thacher is always a leader in private equity. They advise the biggest investment funds on earth. The work is incredibly lucrative, especially when the stock market is booming. They are the textbook definition of elite wall street law firms. If you want to be at the center of the financial universe, this is where you want to be.

### 8\. Weil, Gotshal & Manges

Weil is globally famous for its bankruptcy and corporate restructuring departments. When huge companies run out of money and go bankrupt, Weil manages the chaotic fallout. Their work actually thrives in bad economies, offering unique job stability during recessions. Working at a big law firm in nyc like Weil means you learn exactly how to handle high pressure corporate disasters without flinching.

### 9\. Cleary Gottlieb Steen & Hamilton

Cleary is a pioneer in international law. They represent sovereign nations, entire countries, and massive international wealth funds. You get to travel globally and work on complex, multi nation deals. They also boast a fantastic team of the top real estate law firms New York developers completely trust for massive skyscraper building projects.

### 10\. Cravath, Swaine & Moore

Cravath is the absolute gold standard of the legal industry. Honestly, no list of the biggest law firms in New Yorkis complete without them. They literally set the standard salary scale that every other major firm follows. It is an environment of unmatched prestige and elite training. They also employ brilliant minds working alongside the best intellectual property law firms nyc has to offer.

## FAQs

### What is the starting salary for new lawyers?

The starting salary for big law in NYC is famously high. Most top tier firms follow a standardized compensation scale. First year associates can expect to earn around two hundred and twenty five thousand dollars as their base pay. Adding bonuses can push this much higher at the end of the year. However, you have to earn every single penny of that paycheck. Associates are expected to work incredibly long hours. The financial rewards are massive, but the pressure to perform perfectly is equally intense.

### How do modern law firms find new clients?

Word of mouth is great, but modern practices rely heavily on smart digital marketing to grow. Instead of waiting for the phone to ring, they actively build their online presence. This includes running targeted advertisements and optimizing their websites for search engines to capture high quality traffic. Many firms also focus heavily on building automated funnels to ensure a steady stream of new cases. It is a highly competitive market today. A robust online strategy is absolutely essential for long term growth.

### What are the typical billable hour targets?

The billable hours requirements big law sets are notoriously steep. Most of the biggest law firms in New York expect associates to bill between two thousand and two thousand two hundred hours annually. This does not include administrative tasks or training seminars. It only counts the time actively working on client matters. To hit these targets, lawyers often work late nights and weekends regularly. It is a grueling schedule. This is the primary tradeoff for the high salary and the incredible prestige of the job.

### Do these firms only handle corporate cases?

Not at all. While they are widely known as the top corporate law firms in New York City, they handle much more than just business disputes. Their corporate law practice areas are vast, covering finance, tax, and more. However, they also have elite teams for litigation, real estate, and intellectual property. Some even handle high profile pro bono cases for civil rights issues. They are full service institutions designed to handle any problem a massive global corporation might face in the modern business world.

### How competitive are the summer internships?

Gaining entry into the summer associate programs nyc offers is highly competitive. Thousands of the top law students apply for a handful of spots every single year. Firms typically recruit heavily from the highest ranked law schools in the country. A successful summer associate stint almost always leads to a full time job offer after graduation. Therefore, students fight hard for these coveted positions. The interview process is rigorous and demanding. It requires exceptional grades and incredibly strong interpersonal skills.
